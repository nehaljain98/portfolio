UC Davis Website Redesign

Design Careers Club  is a registered student organisation that hosts student workshops, alumni events and design industry day.

As the President of the Design Careers Club, I designed the website with a talented marketing team over the course of a quarter. Under the supervision of Professor Young, we created wireframes, addressed and considered user profiles, and implemented the best course of action for the club.

Project Details

Role

UX Designer

Timeframe

January - March 2020

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Team

Professor Jyayi Young and Sarrah Solomon

Objective

The entire design department depends on the club to contact alumni and recruiters for the development of design students.

Our objective was to create a multi-purpose website that could continue for the course of the club. Since the club's leadership changes on an annual basis, we decided to use a website building platform such as weebly for the sake of longevity. We discussed coding the website from scratch and inviting developers to the team but this would have worked against the club and the objective of continued legacy of the website.
