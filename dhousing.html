Davis Housing

September 2019 - March 2020

Davis Housing is a website for consumers that provides access to Davis housing locations through an information-based search bar.

As the Product Designer, I led discovery, exploration, definition, and testing of new features to improve customer KPIs so that the website can help customers find the right place to live based on their wants and needs. 

Context

Overview

Unfortunately, the work I did at Davis Housing must remain confidential at this time. What I can say is that I helped lay the foundation for the digital experience of a new information-search engine. I collaborated with a team to build the pilot version of the website that was built and launched for testing: presenting designs, and research to the stakeholders weekly. Additionally, collaborated with the engineering team to ensure my designs were built to spec. You can check out the new Davis Housing website here.
